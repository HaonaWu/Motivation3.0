Our Amazing Team

We are three high school students from Girls Who Code at Moody’s.

Isabelle Greene

Lead Researcher

My name is Isabelle Greene. I am sixteen years old, and I grew up and currently live in the Bronx. My favorite subjects in school are history and math, and I hope to incorporate these subjects together for my undergrad major. I am the Lead Researcher who’s role included gathering research in regards to student resources. My team and I understand that many students are facing stresses about academic works from school, therefore we create this web to provide free resources open to any students who wants to learn freely to make a change.

Magen Chen

Lead Developer

Hello, my name is Magen. I am a rising high school senior from Toronto, Canada, at Cardinal Carter Academy for the Arts, where I major in music. I am a co-founder and lead developer of Motivation Sphere. Motivation Sphere was created for students by students with the purpose of creating a stress-free environment for learning. Thanks to my teammates, we were able to make this happen.

HaoNa Wu

Lead Designer

Hi my name is Haona, a rising high school junior from Long Island, NY. As a co-founder of Motivation Sphere, my role is to design the websites template, web layout and make sure the websites’ functionally meets our need and express our purpose well. By designing a web to become functional, a branch of my role also manage to bring all the ideas together from the team, in which I plan to create something new and capable.
